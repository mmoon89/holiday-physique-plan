# Holiday Physique Plan

A 10-week holiday physique transformation plan focused on:

- Building or maintaining muscle
- Losing body fat sustainably
- Improving conditioning and appearance
- Balancing training with work and family life

## Includes

- 4-day training split
- Nutrition and calorie targets
- High-protein guidance
- Conditioning and steps targets
- Recovery and supplement guidance
- Peak week recommendations

## GitHub Pages

To enable the website:

1. Go to Settings
2. Click Pages
3. Under "Build and deployment", choose:
   - Source: Deploy from a branch
   - Branch: main
   - Folder: /root
4. Save

Your site will then appear at:

https://mmoon89.github.io/holiday-physique-plan/
